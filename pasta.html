Classic Pasta

A delicious and simple pasta recipe perfect for any meal.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

servings

4 servings

Tags: dinner dinner

instructions

step 1

Boil a large pot of salted water and cook pasta according to package instructions.

step 2

In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© garlic until fragrant.

step 3

Add diced tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 10 minutes.

step 4

Drain pasta and mix with the sauce. Toss to coat evenly.

step 5

Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.

ingredients

200g pasta

2 tbsp olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

400g canned diced tomatoes

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tsp dried oregano

Grated Parmesan cheese

Fresh basil leaves

tools

Large pot

Pan

Wooden spoon

Colander

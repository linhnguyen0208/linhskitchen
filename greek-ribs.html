Sweet Sour Pork Ribs

A classic Vietnamese dish with a perfect balance of sweet and sour flavors, combined with tender pork ribs.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : dinner Vietnamese

instructions

step 1

Blanch the pork ribs in boiling water for 5 minutes to remove impurities, then drain and rinse under cold water.

step 2

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the ribs and sear until they turn golden brown on all sides.

step 3

Add sugar to the pan and caramelize it until golden brown. Stir well to coat the ribs in caramelized sugar.

step 4

Pour in vinegar, soy sauce, and water. Stir and bring to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer.

step 5

Cover and let the ribs cook for about 30-40 minutes until tender, stirring occasionally.

step 6

Increase heat to high and reduce the sauce until it thickens and coats the ribs.

step 7

Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions if desired.

ingredients

500g pork ribs

2 tbsp sugar

3 tbsp vinegar

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 cup water

Salt to taste

tools

Knife

Cutting board

Non-stick pan

Spatula

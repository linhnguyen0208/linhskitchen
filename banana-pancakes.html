Banana Pancakes

Delicious and fluffy banana pancakes, perfect for a quick and healthy breakfast!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : breakfast

instructions

step 1

Mash the bananas in a large mixing bowl until smooth.

step 2

Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, then whisk to combine.

step 3

Sift in flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir until just combined.

step 4

Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.

step 5

Pour small amounts of batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.

step 6

Flip and cook for another minute until golden brown.

step 7

Serve warm with syrup, honey, or fresh fruits.

ingredients

2 ripe bananas

2 eggs

1 cup milk

1 tsp vanilla extract

1 cup flour

1 tsp baking powder

1/4 tsp salt

tools

Mixing bowl

Whisk

Non-stick pan

Spatula

Spring Roll

Fresh and healthy Vietnamese spring rolls filled with shrimp, fresh herbs, vermicelli noodles, and wrapped in rice paper.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags: Vietnamese dinner

instructions

step 1

Cook the vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

step 2

Boil the shrimp for 2-3 minutes until pink and fully cooked. Slice them in half lengthwise.

step 3

Prepare a large bowl of warm water to soften the rice paper.

step 4

Dip a sheet of rice paper into the warm water for a few seconds until pliable.

step 5

Place lettuce, vermicelli noodles, herbs, and shrimp on the rice paper.

step 6

Fold in the sides and roll tightly from the bottom to the top.

step 7

Serve immediately with hoisin peanut sauce or fish sauce dip.

ingredients

8 rice paper sheets

1/2 lb shrimp

4 oz vermicelli noodles

1 cup lettuce

1/2 cup fresh mint leaves

1/2 cup fresh cilantro

tools

Large Pot

Strainer

Cutting Board

Sharp Knife

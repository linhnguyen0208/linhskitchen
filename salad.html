Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar Salad with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a creamy, tangy dressing. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal!

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Lunch Dinner

instructions

step 1

Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly. Tear it into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.

step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together the Caesar dressing ingredients: mayonnaise, grated Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.

step 3

Add the croutons to the salad bowl. Drizzle the dressing over the lettuce and croutons, then toss gently to coat everything evenly.

step 4

Sprinkle additional grated Parmesan cheese on top and serve immediately.

ingredients

1 large head of romaine lettuce

1 cup croutons

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1/4 cup mayonnaise

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

1 clove garlic, minced

Salt and pepper to taste

tools

Large Salad Bowl

Whisk

Measuring Spoons

Knife and Cutting Board

Classic Sandwich

A simple and delicious sandwich perfect for any meal of the day.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : breakfast

instructions

step 1

Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

step 2

Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.

step 3

Layer lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, and ham/turkey on one slice.

step 4

Place the second slice of bread on top and press gently.

step 5

Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.

ingredients

2 slices of bread

1 tbsp butter or mayonnaise

2 lettuce leaves

2 slices of tomato

2 slices of cheese

2 slices of ham or turkey

tools

Toaster

Knife

Cutting Board

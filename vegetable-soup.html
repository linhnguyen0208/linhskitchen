Vegetable Soup

A hearty and healthy vegetable soup, perfect for any season.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

servings

6 servings

Tags: dinner

instructions

step 1

Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, and celery. Saut√© until softened.

step 2

Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

step 3

Pour in vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, and bring to a boil.

step 4

Add potatoes and green beans, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.

step 5

Stir in corn, peas, and season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Cook for another 5 minutes.

step 6

Serve hot with fresh parsley garnish.

ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil

1 onion, chopped

2 carrots, sliced

2 celery stalks, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

4 cups vegetable broth

1 can diced tomatoes

2 potatoes, cubed

1 cup green beans, chopped

1/2 cup corn

1/2 cup peas

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tsp dried basil

Fresh parsley for garnish

tools

Large pot

Wooden spoon

Knife

Cutting board

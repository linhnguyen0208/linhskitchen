Scrambled Eggs

A simple and delicious scrambled eggs recipe. Perfect for breakfast or a quick meal.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : breakfast

instructions

step 1

Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and whisk well.

step 2

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and melt the butter.

step 3

Pour the eggs into the pan and gently stir with a spatula until softly set.

ingredients

2 large eggs

1 tbsp butter

Salt & pepper to taste

tools

Mixing bowl

Whisk

Non-stick pan

Spatula
